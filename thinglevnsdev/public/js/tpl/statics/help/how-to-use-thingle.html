How to sign up for Thingle?

Signing up for Thingle is easy! Just register or log in via Facebook by clicking on the log in button on the top right hand corner.

Thingle is never going to sell or hand over your personal information to anyone else.

How to create a thingle with the +thingle bookmarklet?

  • Install the +thingle bookmarklet
  • Add a thingle using the +thingle bookmarklet

Install the +thingle bookmarklet

  1. First, in case it is not already there, make sure the bookmark bar for your browser is visible. It will appear above the URL address bar:

    For Chrome: Click the icon on the browser toolbar and select Bookmarks. Then, select the option to always show bookmarks bar.

    For Safari: Click View > Show Bookmarks Bar

    For Firefox: Click View > Toolbars > Bookmarks Toolbar

  2. Second, Click and drag this +thingle into the bookmark bar

Add a thingle using the +thingle bookmarklet

  1. While on a page showing an image of something you would like to add, simply click on the +thingle bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar

  2. You will see a list of images the bookmarklet has found on the page.

  3. Click on the image you would like to use for your new thingle, a new window will open.

  4. Add a title and a description to your thingle to let others know what your thingle is about.

  5. Choose the collection where you would like to have your new thingle added to and click on Save.

  6. Great - your thingle has been created! You can now view the thingle and / or add more photos to it.

How can I change the title of a thingle?

  1. Navigate to the thingle you would like to edit

  2. Click on the edit icon p next to the title the thingle.

  3. When you are done with your changes, click on the button to save your changes.

How can I change the description of a thingle?

  1. Navigate to the thingle you would like to edit

  2. Click on the edit icon p next to “Description”.

  3. Click on Submit to save your changes or Cancel Cancel to discard your changes.

How to add a collection?

Click on the “Create button” in the top right corner of the page and follow the steps. As soon as you have added a collection it’s available for you on the shelf and when adding/creating or collecting new thingles.

How to collect thingles?

Click on the icon + on the thingle and choose your collection or just open your shelf, drag and drop it onto the collection you wish to add it to.

How to follow people?

Click on the person on the thingle and hit the “follow button” Follow beneath the user’s picture. If you want to unfollow, just click on the button again.
